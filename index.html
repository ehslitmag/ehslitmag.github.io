About

The EHS Literary Magazine is a student-run online periodical that publishes various types of artistic mediums every quarter.

During Lit Mag meetings, we collect and revise submissions and take our time to write and share our own written pieces.

We meet every Monday from 2:00pm to 2:45pm in A216.

Terms of Submission

Submissions must be a short story, photograph, poem, essay, or piece of artwork.

Submissions must:

  1. Include your name
  2. Be school appropriate
    • Be respectful of any culture, religion, or orientation
    • No profanity
    • No violence
    • No sexual themes

      • Any submission that contains inappropriate language or themes will be edited and censored.

  3. Be less than 2500 words for a writing piece
  4. Be at least 500 pixels big for an image or an image of artwork
  5. Provide an URL and be credited if a writing piece includes an outside image

Submissions can:

  1. Be anonymous (explicitly state this in your submission)
  2. Include several submissions per email
Submit to ehsliterarymag@gmail.com
