About

The EHS Literary Magazine is a student-run periodical that publishes various types of artistic mediums every quarter.

We are currently deciding the format of the upcoming magazine.

Meetings are every Monday from 2:00pm to 2:45pm in A216.

Terms of Submission

Submissions must be a short story, photograph, poem, essay, or piece of artwork.

Submissions must:

  1. Include your name
  2. Be school appropriate
    • Be respectful of culture, religion, or orientation
    • No profanity
    • No inappropriate topics

      • Any submission that includes inappropriate language or themes will be edited and censored

      • Be less than 2500 words for a writing piece
      • Be at least 500 pixels big for an image or an image of artwork
      • Provide an URL and be credited if a writing piece includes an outside image

        • Submissions can:

      • Be anonymous (Explicitly state this in your submission)
      • Include several submissions per email
        Submit to ehsliterarymag@gmail.com
